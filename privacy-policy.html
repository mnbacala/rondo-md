Privacy Policy

www.drgiavonnerondo.com uses Google Analytics.

Google Analytics is a piece of software that tracks data about our visitors.

What does Google Analytics record?

  • What website you came from to get here
  • How long you stay for
  • What kind of device you are using

What do we do with your data?

The tracking information allows GObileMD PLLC to better understand the kind of people who come to the website and what content they are reading. This allows GObileMD PLLC to make better decisions about design and content.

Occasionally, we will compile aggregate statistics about the number of visitors this site receives and browsers being used. No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting.

All of my activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service.

Want to opt out of tracking?

You can use a browser plugin to opt out of all Google Analytics tracking software.
