Pricing

At GObileMD, we are membership-based, each plan containing an array of services. There are three different plans: the Basic plan, the GObileMD plan, and the DPtimal, or optimal plan. Plans start at $85 per month. Members can choose to pay either annually or monthly. In addition to plans, we also offer single visits to our office at a cost of $125 upfront.

COVID-19 Update
